ABOUT ME

I am Yuxi Zhang (张羽兮), a graduate student major in Computer Science at University of California, Los Angeles.

I got my Bachelor's degree in Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My primary major is Computer Science & Technology with a second major in Musicology.

My interested fields include machine learning, artificial intelligence, computer vision and computer graphics. Also, I love playing the violin as well as composing music.
