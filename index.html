Computer-GO AI

  • Time: Fall 2013, Junior year
  • Course: Artificial Intelligence
  • Lecturer: Liqing Zhang
  • Other group members: Yuxian Qiu, Kaihua Tang, Weijian Cao, Yao Cheng
  • Purpose: Design an AI program for Computer-GO game.
  • Algorithms: Min-Max search, α-β cut, Upper bound for Confidence Tree (UCT), Statistical Evaluation on Random Simulation (SERS)
  • Implement Platform & Language: GOGUI, C
  • Demo: This demo shows two versions of our AI programs, SERS version (white) vs. Min-Max search version (black), playing with each other.
     
    •  
  • Introduction to SERS: This is an original algorithm designed by our group. This algorithm combines the advantanges of Min-Max search and UCT and meanwhile avoids their defects. For each step, this algorithm firstly randomly plays several steps and evaluates the situation with a cost function. Afther a large quantity of random simulations, it finally picks the step with the best evaluation results. We perform simulations on a confidence tree and use α-β cut to accelerate the searching speed.
