Research

Research interests

My research interests include artificial intelligence, machine learning applications, data mining, neural and cognitive computing and music information retrieval.

My ultimate research goal is to design strong artificial intelligence. I want to approach this purpose through understanding principle of brain operation. Also, I am very interested in applications of machine learning methods, a more practical field. I have a strong willing to apply machine learning methods to analyzing neural signals and recognizing human thoughts, which sounds like magic. In addition, music is my avocation, in which I also want to use machine learning methods to extract meaningful information.

I am now working on Brain-Computer Interface in Brain-like Computing & Machine Intelligence (BCMI) Lab in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Liqing Zhang.

  • Institute: Big Data & Business Innovation Lab, Shanghai Jiao Tong University
